Chinmay B Sabarad

Head of Traffic & Transportation Systems

"Traffic isn't just cars; it's a flow network. By applying advanced graph algorithms, we minimize congestion and reduce the city's carbon footprint."

10 Key Problems Solved

Dijkstra's Algo

Shortest Path Navigation

Calculating the fastest route for EVs from point A to B considering dynamic edge weights (traffic).

Ford-Fulkerson

Max Traffic Flow

Determining the maximum capacity of the highway network during rush hour to prevent bottlenecks.

BFS

Subway Connectivity Check

Using Breadth-First Search to ensure all stations in the metro network are reachable from the central hub.

Cycle Detection

Deadlock Prevention

Detecting circular dependencies in autonomous vehicle routing logic using DFS to prevent gridlock.

Union-Find

Road Network Maintenance

Determining connected components of the city road map when specific roads are closed for repairs.

Tarjan's Algo

Critical Bridge Identification

Finding articulation points in the graph to identify bridges that, if failed, would disconnect the city.

Sorting

Traffic Light Sync

Sorting intersection throughput data to prioritize green light duration for high-volume streets.

A* Search

Drone Delivery Routing

Heuristic pathfinding for aerial delivery drones to avoid no-fly zones and high buildings.

Sliding Window

Congestion Monitoring

Analyzing real-time sensor streams to detect traffic spikes within a specific time window.

Greedy

EV Charging Stations

Optimally placing charging stations to cover the maximum number of road segments with minimum cost.

Amogh Samji

Head of Energy & Utilities

"Sustainable power requires smart distribution. We use greedy algorithms and spanning trees to ensure every watt produced by our solar farms is used efficiently."

10 Key Problems Solved

Prim's Algorithm

Grid Connectivity

Designing the Minimum Spanning Tree (MST) for cabling to connect all neighborhoods with the least amount of copper.

Knapsack (DP)

Battery Storage Optimization

Deciding which energy packets to store in batteries during peak production to maximize value during night time.

Greedy

Load Balancing

Assigning power requests to the nearest available substation to minimize transmission loss.

Topological Sort

System Boot Sequence

Determining the order to turn on power plant subsystems where certain systems depend on others.

Binary Search

Fault Detection

Quickly locating a break in a long linear power line by testing midpoints.

Segment Tree

Peak Usage Query

Efficiently querying the maximum energy usage in a specific district over a given time range.

Huffman Coding

Smart Meter Compression

Compressing data sent from smart meters to the central hub to save bandwidth.

Graph Coloring

Frequency Allocation

Assigning control frequencies to smart substations so adjacent stations don't interfere.

Fenwick Tree

Real-time Consumption

Updating and calculating prefix sums of energy consumption across city blocks dynamically.

Hash Map

Billing & User lookup

O(1) access to user profiles and energy credits for millions of citizens.

Subhash Reddy

Head of Urban Planning

"A city is a matrix. I use Dynamic Programming and Spatial Data Structures to ensure optimal land use, minimizing sprawl and maximizing green space."

10 Key Problems Solved

2D Prefix Sum

Population Density Analysis

Rapidly calculating the total population in any rectangular region of the city grid.

Quadtree

Spatial Indexing

Efficiently storing locations of points of interest (parks, schools) to query "what is near me".

Convex Hull

City Boundary Logic

Determining the smallest convex polygon containing all city suburbs for administrative zoning.

Dynamic Programming

Housing Allocation

Optimizing the mix of affordable, luxury, and commercial units to maximize utility under budget constraints.

Backtracking

Constraint Satisfaction

Solving zoning puzzles (e.g., placing industrial zones far from residential ones) by exploring valid configurations.

Stack

Skyline Problem

Computing the city skyline view for rendering and sunlight analysis using monotonic stacks.

K-Means Clustering

Service Center Locations

Grouping neighborhoods to determine the optimal centroids for new post offices or libraries.

Bipartite Matching

Job Assignment

Matching construction crews to building projects based on skills and location constraints.

Line Sweep

Overlap Detection

Detecting overlapping structures or utility lines in the architectural blueprints.

Matrix Traversal (DFS)

Flood Risk Assessment

Simulating water flow across the city terrain grid to identify low-lying flood zones.

Abhinav Raj

Head of Emergency & Waste Services

"Safety and cleanliness rely on speed. We utilize Heaps and Priority Queues to ensure ambulances arrive instantly and waste is collected before bins overflow."

10 Key Problems Solved

Priority Queue (Min-Heap)

Emergency Dispatch

Always dispatching the nearest ambulance to the highest severity incident first.

TSP (Approximation)

Garbage Truck Routing

Optimizing the route for waste collection trucks to visit all bins and return to the depot with minimum fuel.

Multi-Source BFS

Fire Station Coverage

Calculating the minimum time for fire trucks to reach any building from multiple station locations simultaneously.

K-Nearest Neighbors

Crime Hotspot Prediction

Classifying safe vs. risk zones based on proximity to historical incident data.

Trie

Emergency Contact Lookup

Autocompleting and finding medical records or addresses instantly during 911 calls.

Interval Scheduling

Hospital Bed Management

Maximizing the number of surgeries scheduled in operating rooms based on start/end times.

Circular Queue

Dispatch Logs

Maintaining a fixed-size buffer of the most recent emergency calls for audit and playback.

Floyd-Warshall

Disaster Evacuation

Pre-calculating all-pairs shortest paths to determine evacuation routes from every district to every shelter.

Set (HashSet)

Duplicate Incident Filtering

Filtering out multiple reports of the same fire or accident in real-time to prevent resource waste.

LRU Cache

Medical Data Access

Caching recently accessed patient records on ambulance tablets for instant retrieval.
